Anti-Fit Is the New Skinny!

The secret to a great butt is . . . droopy denim? How’s that for a great American fashion eclipse of 2017? Call it slouchy, call it workwear, just don’t call it the boyfriend jean.

  • email
August 30, 2017 Fashion

By Sally Singer

Video by Cass Bird

Edited by Jorden Bickham

  • email
Share this story

During the last gasp of summer, Cass Bird and Jorden Bickham headed to Long Island with a quartet of model pals (Andreea Diaconu, Mia Kang, Paloma Elsesser, Sasha Kichigina), a bunch of crocheted bikinis and tiny tanks, strings of shells, and a stack of the season’s most covetable relaxed denim. Those jeans and jackets, which here outfitted a little fun in the all-too-present sun, will also serve as your wardrobe staples post–Labor Day, as the silhouette loosens up shoulder to toe. Rejoice! In this one regard—the pulling on and zipping up—we can all breathe easier.
