How I Style My Jeans for Date Night

This series is brought to you by Levi’s® Curve ID
Find your custom fit at Levi’s® stores or Levi.com

Although I love getting dressed up, my husband isn’t a big fan of it (to put it mildly).  So when we do get a night out alone, we typically choose more laid-back spots where we can share appetizers/drinks and people-watch.  But, my philosophy is…just because you are going somewhere more casual doesn’t mean you can’t look pulled together.  So, I thought I’d share with you my favorite way to style my Levi’s® jeans for a date night out.  It’s a no-fail combination that is both comfortable & stylish.  All you need are great-fitting skinny jeans, pointy-toe pumps, a silky blazer & a dash of sequins!

What I’m wearing:
Lauren Conrad blazer (on sale here), J. Crew sequin tank (via eBay), Levi’s® skinny jeans (find your Curve ID here), Mossimo pumps (here), Forever 21 clutch, Accessorize necklace, J. Crew & Target bracelets, Lips: Cover Girl Temptress
