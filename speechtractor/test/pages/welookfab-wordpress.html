Angie Challenge Day 1: FFBO (handknit sweater, jeans, combat boots)

Diana
Veteran

Today's Angie Challenge entry is a FFBO, featuring my favorite sweater, perfect jeans, and combat boots.

Why this outfit is very "me":
--The handknit sweater obviously. This cream cabled one is probably my favorite out of all of them; it's certainly the one I wear the most.
--Jeans. I wear jeans several times a week, except in the summer. They're perfect for my life and work. Not to brag, but although I find it difficult to find jeans that fit, once I do get the perfect fitting ones I think they tend to look great on me. These Rag and Bone jeans from NAS a couple of years ago are some of my favorites.
--black midcalf combat boots. Possibly my favorite style of shoe. I love mid calf boots (still show some leg but tall enough that pants don't bag and bunch), but they are hard to find. These are new (to replace some old, dead ones that I tossed when I moved). See find below.
--piles of handknit outerwear (hat, gloves, scarf). I don't wear puffers but all this wool keeps me plenty warm!

Bonus outfit (#5) is from last week that I never posted. It features the same sweater with crazy Liberty print teal pants and teal combat boots. Teal is my favorite color and I love crazy pants and Liberty prints in particular. Unfortunately I think these Liberty jeans are on their last legs as the material is wearing very thin in some places.
